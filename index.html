Diamonds — Rules

How to play

Place a bet, then reveal tiles on the grid. Each diamond you find increases your multiplier. Uncover a trap and you lose your bet. Cash out at any time to claim your current payout.

Multiplier formula

Multiplier = 0.99 × C(N, k) ÷ C(N−M, k), where N = total tiles, M = traps, k = diamonds found. Each pick raises the multiplier by adjusting for survival probability.
