Obsidian Mines — Rules

How to play

Place a bet, then reveal tiles on the grid. Each safe tile increases your multiplier. Hit a mine and you lose. Cash out at any time to claim your current payout.

Multiplier formula

Multiplier = 0.99 × C(N, k) ÷ C(N−M, k), where N = total tiles, M = mines, k = gems revealed. Each pick raises the multiplier by adjusting for survival probability.
